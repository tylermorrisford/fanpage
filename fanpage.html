Color Animal Rocks

Color Animal is an SLC-based garage-rock quartet that has released four studio albums to date, with the most recent being the studio recordings of songs written for a collaboration with the Municipal Ballet Co., titled 'Night'.

About the release of 'Night', the band recently posted:

"Night" has arrived! Huge heaps of thanks to Sarah Olivia Longoria and Municipal Ballet Co. for their collaboration â€” the album is the music half of the ballet we created together last year. Big thanks to Michael Sasich at Man v Music for the amazing recording. Also thanks to Evan Jed Memmott for the gorgeous artwork, Chad Kirkland and Hillary Musgrove-Kirkland from Commonwealth Studios for hosting the ballet last year (and for taking incredible photos of the ballet). And thanks to YOU for reading, listening, and showing up!

NIGHT was developed as a collaboration between Municipal Ballet Co. and Color Animal. The ballet was performed on June 8 & 9, 2018, at Commonwealth Studios in South Salt Lake. Choreography by Jo Blake, Hannah Bowcutt, Sara Borazan, Sarah Longoria, Nora Price, and Chase Wise. Performed by Hannah Bowcutt, Amy Falls, Mary Jessie Floor, Sarah Lund, Ursula Perry, Nora Price, Stacie Riskin, Tristana Yegge, and Joni Wilson. Juggling by David Ayala and Tim Dwyer. Film by Ben Estabrook. Special thanks to Hillary Musgrove-Kirkland, Chad Kirkland, Cody Kirkland, Commonwealth Studios, and Fisher Beer.

You can stream "Night" on Spotify, Apple Music, Amazon Unlimited, or practically any other streaming service you can imagine.
